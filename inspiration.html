THIS IS MY STORY:


I started my creative journey 2017. At a doctors appointment I was bored, so I got a paper and a pen and started to doodle, while he was talking. I found it very relaxing and that drawing bebcame a new chapter in my life. In that hospital I found a new hobby that became a very importend part of my life. I have always been fascinated by faces so I developed my own teqniqe doing faces- with lots of color and flowers.
Since that day I have sold many paintings and I had exhibitions all over Europe.
During the pandemic I had an oppertunity to open my own art Gallery in Täby Centrum, the biggest mall in Sweden.
Everybody carry a lifestory and everybody deserve to tell it. I tell my story throu my artworks- I i wish more people find this painting as a channel to develiope their creativity.
